Education

Saint Louis University: M.S. in Sustainability

2013 - 2015

My emphases were in alternative transportation research, statistical analysis, and GIS, but other major themes of the program included environmental communication, research design, and reporting. For my capstone project, I designed and implemented a bicycle network assessment in St. Louis, MO. To generate data, I surveyed over 90 local cyclists about how much stress they felt on their daily bike routes. I then collected and synthesized large sets of street and traffic data from various local organizations. Through statistical and spatial analysis, I was able to map high- and low-stress bicycle routes throughout the study area and determine which environmental variables were correlated with cyclist stress. I presented my study in December 2015 and am currently editing my paper for publication.
